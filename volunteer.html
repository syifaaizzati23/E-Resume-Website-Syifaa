THE MEMORIES OF US

  • I had the opportunity to attend the Malaysian Library Annual Conference 2024 at the Albukhary International University. Together with several of my classmates, I participated as a volunteer during the event. I gained a lot of valuable knowledge from the people around me and from new friends I met at the conference. I am truly grateful for the chance to meet many inspiring and knowledgeable individuals there, and I created some wonderful memories with good people. During the conference, I learned many new things, such as managing the booths and posters, assisting librarians in preparing certificates, and organizing the necessary gifts for participants. I also gained new experiences and knowledge by observing and interacting with the librarians. Through these activities, my classmates and I were able to acquire a lot of practical knowledge in this field, and we felt confident engaging with the librarians and learning directly from their expertise.
